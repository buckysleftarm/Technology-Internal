A young FBI trainee by the name of Clarice Sterling has been sent to Baltimore Hospital for the insane to interview one Hannibal Lector. Hannibal was once a very sucessful psychiatrist turned serial killer. As she makes sure to trust her instincts ans not to be swayed by Hannibals tricks, she must make sure she gets all the information that is needed for her to save a woman who was kidnapped by the next psycho "Buffalo Bill".
