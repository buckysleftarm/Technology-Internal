After the death of his father, the King T'Chaka. His only son Prince T'Challa has recently returned back to his home of Wakanda is Africa. Wakanda is an extremly technically advanced but very isolated from the rest of the world. The city is guarded by the Black Panther, a noble warrior postion that is carried by the Wakandan King. But because of the death of the King, T'Challa must now step up and take his place and not only become King but the Black Panther as well. But when somone from his fathers past shows up, T'Challa must fight to prove that he is worthy and ready to become King. But could this person be the downfall of the Black Panther?
