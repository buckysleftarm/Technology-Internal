Cher Horowitz and her best friend Dionne are two attractive sophmores who live in Beverly Hills. Recently, Cher had recived a bad grade from a particular moody teacher, so the girls try to set him up with another teacher to make him happier... and to hopefully bring up her grade. One day a gril named Tai transfers to their school, Cher and Dionne take Tai under their wing. The girls give Tai a makeover and to hopefully get her a boyfriend. But at the same time, Cher is hoping to get herself a boyfriend. After a long time of searching, Cher seems to not be able to find a boy that is god enough to date. After many mistakes, disasters and many miscommunications, Cher eventually finds the boy of her dreams.
