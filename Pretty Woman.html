Vivian Ward is a woman who has made a career for herself by working as a protitute in Hollywood Boulevard. One day she runs into the man that she has been dreaming of, Edward Lewis. He is a very wealthy businessman who at first does not believe that Vivian could be any more than a girl from the street. However, Vivian proves him wrong when she becomes the beautiful stranger that somehow makes Edward fall in love with her. Throughout their complicated relationship, Edward starts to change his ways and Vivian gets the opportunity start her life over again.
