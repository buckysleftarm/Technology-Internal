Famed candy man, Willy Wonka, has released five golden tickets into the world for five lucky people to find. What at first seemed like a harmless game turned into a mad frenzy as every person around the world desperatly wants a ticket. The lucky winners of these tickets will win a tour around Wonkas famed Chocolate Factory. Young Charlie Bucket was lucky enough to be one of the people to have found a ticket. Charlie, along with his grandpa, Joe, tag along with the other lucky recipients on the wild journey around the mysterious chocolate factory.
