A novelist by the name of Jack Torrance has recently undergone a job interview at an old but beautiful hotel called the Overlook Hotel. With him, Jack brings his wife Wendy and son Danny. But unbeknownst to them, Danny has a power which is called 'The Shining'. On their first day at the hotel, Danny meets the cook named Hallorann who warns him of what is lurking in room 237 and around the rest of the hotel. As the days pass them by, Danny starts having more visions, Jack starts starts turning to instanity and madness while Wendy thinks Jack may do something bad.
