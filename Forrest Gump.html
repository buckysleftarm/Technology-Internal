Forrest Gump is a man who has an IQ that is lower than the average person. Throughout his youth, Forrest has been taught by his mother to live his life to the fullest and to follow his dreams or 'destiny'. During the course of his life, Gump has been on many different adventures such as serving in Vietnam, creating a ping pong craze, inspiring people around him to start jogging and many more things. However, to Forrest, none of the amazing things he has done is important to him. All he can think of is his childhood sweetheart, Jenny Curran and hopes to one day be reunited with her again.
