One of Englands best archers, Robin Hood, has made it his mission to free his village from the heartless Prince Johns wrath and from the sherrif of Nottingham. Robin, with his trusty friend Little John, have been going on many secret heists to steal gold from the royals and to give it to the poor. But the one thing Robin Hood wants more is to wed the maid, Marian. But this is not an eaasy feat, as the prince has decided that Robin Hood shall be arrested. The people of the village have decided amongst themselves that they will not stand for Robin being arrested so, they decided to get King Richard back into the throne. Together, Robin Hood and Little John fight for their village and get rid of Prince John and the sherrif of Nottingham.
