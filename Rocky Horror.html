On a particulary rainy night, newly engaged couple Brad Majors and Janet Weiss, have become lost on their way back home due to the said rain. So, the two pull over at the first house they come across, one that holds many secrets. Together, the two discover a transvestite named Frank-N-Furter and his many Transylvanian friends. The young couple find themselves in the middle of Frank-N-Furters party where throughout the course of the night Frank-N-Furter has been trying to attract both Janet and Brad.
