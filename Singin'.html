Set back in 1927, Don Lockwood and Lina Lamont are known as a famous on-screen couple. However, Lina takes their on-screen romance as reality. When their latest project is turned into a musical, Don manages to sing the songs perfectly, but Lina does not. After trying and failing with a singing coach, it is decided that Lina will be dubbed using the voice of young actress Kathy Selden. On meeting Kathy, Don accidentaly falls in love with her.
