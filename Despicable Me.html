In a suburbian neighbourhood, there is a tall black house that sits on dead grass. But unbeknownst to the surrounding neighbours, it happens to be the lair of the one and only Gru. Gru is a nefarious criminal who is currently planning his biggest heist ever, with the help of his yellow minions though. His next heist: stealing the moon. As he spends the following days preparing for the upcoming mission. But one day, Gru meets 3 orphan girls while they attempt to sell him cookies. But when he rejects them, he quickly regrets his decision fairly quickly. This is because they move onto his enemy: Vector. So, in a desperate attempt to one-up Vector, he adopts the girls, Margo, Edith and Agnes, into his home.
