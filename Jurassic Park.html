Due to the scientific technology becoming more and more advanced, a man has recently created an island full of dinosaurs. One day, a man named John Hammond has decided to invite two of his grandchildren and four others onto the newly created island. But as an employee attempts to steal some of the dinosaur embryos, all of the security systems get shut down, making the people on the island stuck there. Now they must fight to stay alive and get of the island, without being caught by a dino. Welcome, to Jurassic Park.
