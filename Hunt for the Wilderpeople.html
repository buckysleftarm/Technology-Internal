In rural New Zealand, couple Bella and Hector have recently adopted troubled kid Ricky Baker. Ricky, is a kid from the city who has had many run-ins with the law. Over time Ricky has managed to grow closer to Bella but Hector is having trouble getting close to Ricky. However, after Bellas unexpected death, Ricky starts having a hard time ajusting. Now Hector and Ricky seem to be having even more trouble getting to know one another. After all that had happened, Child Services has decided to take Ricky back to the orphanage. But in a haste to get away, Ricky runs away into the forest. This ingintes a a country wide manhunt for both Hector and Ricky.
