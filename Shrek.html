One day, an ogre named Shrek has discovered that Lord Farquaad has dumped all different sorts of fairytale creatures into his swamp. So, Shrek sets out to go to Lord Farquaads castle to persaude him into giving Shrek his swamp back. But along with him, Shrek is made to bring a loud and seemingly obnoxious donkey, named Donkey. But instead of getting his swamp back, Farquaad instead strikes a deal with Shrek. Shrek must be sent to retrive the Princess Fiona from her heavily guarded tower and present her to the Lord. But on their journey back, Shrek discovers that he might be starting to fall in love with the beautiful princess. But what will happen when appearences are not all that they seem.
