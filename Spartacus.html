About a century before Jesus, the Roman Empire was at large. Spratacus is the story of a man, named Spartacus, who was born and raised into slavery. He was sold to a man who ran a school for gladiators, Lentulus Batiatus. After weeks of being put through intense training, he turns on the people who bought him and led the other slaves into a rebellion.
