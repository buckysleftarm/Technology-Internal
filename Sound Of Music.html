Ever since she was a young child, Maria had longed tp become a nun. Except now that she is older, Maria had discovered that it was not all that it is meant to be. Maria is often getting into trouble and doing the wrong things, so she is sent to work for a retired naval, Captain Georg Von Trapp to care for his seven children. She learns that the children need someone to love and care for them and for some love in their lives. Aside from teaching the kids to sing, Maria and Georg find themselves falling in love. However there is one thing standing in their way: Georg is engaged. Will Maria and the Captain ever get to be with each other?
