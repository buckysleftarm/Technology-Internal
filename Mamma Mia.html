Sophie Sheridan is a 20-year-old girl who lives with her mother, Donna Sheridan on the Greek Island of Kalokairi. Sophie is soon to be married to her fiancée Sky and the only thing that she wishes for is for her father to walk her down the isle. So, Sophie goes behind her mothers back and invites all three of her possible fathers to her wedding. With this Sophie seems to think that she will be able to spot her father immediately. In the meantime, Donna is trying to run a hotel on the island. While it is not turning out successfully, her friends Rosie and Tanya arrive for the wedding and help her get back on track of her life. But with an unexpected reunion with her three ex-lovers, Sam, Bill and Harry, Donna becomes fustrated and annoyed with their appareance. Meanwhile all three of the men think that they are Sophies biological father and Sky, Sophies fiancée is fed up with all of the nonsense.
