Marty McFly is your average american teenager who's life is not going so well at the moment. His parents are heading on a downward spiral with his mother becoming a alcoholic and his father being tormented by his supervisor. One day Marty gets a call from his mad scientist friend, Doctor 'Doc' Emmet Brown, who tells him to at the mall in the early hours of the morning. There Doc reveals to Marty a DeLorean that he has transformed into a time machine. During a trial of this newest creation, Marty and Doc get thrust back into the 50s. There he accidently meddles in his teenage parents relationship from blooming. Together Marty and Doc Brown must find a way to reunite Martys parents and make their way back into their time. Will they succeed in time?
