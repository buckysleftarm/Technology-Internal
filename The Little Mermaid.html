Ariel, a rebellious 16-year-old mermaid, has taken a great facsination with life on land. During one of her most recent visits to the surface, Ariel saves and falls in love with Prince Eric. However, Ariels' father King Triton, is not pleased with this action as going to the surface is forbidden. Desperate to see the dashing prince again, Ariel makes a deal with the wicked sea-witch, Ursula. This deal grants her the ability to walk on land for three days. But it comes with a price. Ariel may have her legs but loose the ability to talk for the duration of that time. As things go well on the surface for tthe two lovers, will all go well under the sea?
