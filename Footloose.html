A teenager from the city Chicago of moves to a small town Bomont where all Rock'n'Roll and dancing have been banned. Ren tries his hardest to make sure he fits in in this strange town. But soon he finds himself in deep trouble when he falls in love with the preachers daughter, Ariel Moore. Ren ends up trying to convince the town council to lift the ban on dancing and win over the preacher through his dance moves.
