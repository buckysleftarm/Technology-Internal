Cady Heron has recently moved to America from Africa. When she had lived in Africa, Cady had spent her life being homeschooled, but now that she is in America, Cady will now have to attend high school. On her first day she meets her new friends Janis and Damian, who warn her to stay away from the Plastics. The Plastics are the most popular girls at the school and are made up of three incredibly beautiful girls. Gretchen Wieners, Karen Smith and their leader, Regina George. But one day, Cady sets her eyes on Aaron Samuels and instantly falls in love with him. Except theres one things in the way, Aaron is Reginas ex-boyfriend. So when Regina finds out, she seeks revenge on Cady by get back with Aaron. However, as Cady starts to spend more and more time with the Plastics, she starts to become just like them.
