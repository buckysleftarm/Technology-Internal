Three scientists or 'professors', Peter Venkman, Ray Stanz and Egon Spengler, have been experimenting and researching the paranormal with their students. This experimentation leads to the men out of work. As a result of this, it leads to the men creating their own business inside of an abandoned fire station. One day, when a skyscraper downtown was taken over by one of these paranormal beings, the men who have been dubbed the 'Ghostbusters' are on the case along with their catchy theme song.
