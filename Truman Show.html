Truman Burbank is an ordinary man who lives an ordinary life. He is a very good business man and has a very happy marriage with a woman named Meryl Burbank. At first he is very happy with his life but as time goes by, Truman starts to find that his life is gettign very repetitive. All Truman wants is to be able to leave his town and go to explore the world. But that is not going to happen. Truman starts noticing a lot of suspicious things that are happening around him. As it turns out, Trumans life is being documented for the rest of the world to watch. It has become a very successful franchise dubbed, 'The Truman Show'. He discovers that the twon he is living on is actually a Hollywood set and that all the people around him, including his wife, are paid actors and actresses. With this newfound information, Truman does what he thinks is best, and runs away from set. Will he make it out in time?
