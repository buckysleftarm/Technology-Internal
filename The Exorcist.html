Successfull actress Chris MacNeil and her pre-teen daughter Regan, have recently relocated in Washington DC. Over time Chris notices that her daughter has started showing some disturbing behaviour after coming into contact with a supernatural being. As a result of this interaction, Regan has become more and more violent by the day. Chris is trying everything in she can to help her daughter but nothing seems to be working. So, Chris turns to a priest by the name of Father Damien Karras and exorcist Father Lankester as a last resort. The two fathers perform a ritual ot rid Regan of the evil being. Will she fianlly be free are haunted forever?
