A weather man by the name of Phil Connors is sent a story about a so-called weather forecasting 'rat'. This day marks the fourth year that he has been spending on this story and Phils anger is getting the better of him. The next day, Phil awakens to find that everything that he was doing that day is the same things that he had done the previous day. The cycle continues repeating over and over again for Phil, creating a Groundhog Day. At first, uses that groundhog day to his advantage and makes the same arrogant mistakes he had made before but then realises that these actions will not undo this. Finding himself doomed to this new reality, will Phil ever be able to change his ways?
