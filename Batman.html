Dynamic duo, Batman and Robin, were called to rescue Commodore Schmidlapp, but they were not aware of the fact that they were walking straight into a trap. Schmidlapp had been kidnapped by Gothams biggest criminals: the Joker, the Penguin, the Riddler and Catwoman. The foursome are armed with a dehydrator that can turn humans into dust and they have intentions to take over the world. Its up too Batman and Robin to rescue the Commodore and put a stop to the four criminals plans for world domination.
