Captain Jack Sparrow is a savvy pirate and the captain of the legendary ship, The Black Pearl. Jack has been enlisted by Will Turner to help him find and save the love of his life, Elizabeth Swann. Elizabeth is the governors daughter who has been kidnapped by Jacks enemy Captain Barbossa and his crew. But what Jack and Will don't realise is that Barbossa and his crew have been cursed with immortality. The only cure for the curse is a blood sacrifice and the blood they want? Elizabeth Swann.
