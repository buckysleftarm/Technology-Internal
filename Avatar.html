On the alien planet of Pandora live a highly evolved race called the Na'vi. The Na'vi are giant blue creatures who could resemble humans if they weren't blue. Because of the toxicity of Pandora, a few selected humans must use some Na'vi/human hybrids and connect into them through their minds. Jake Sully, paralyzed ex-Marine, is one of these people. Jake is filling in for his twin brother Tom who is now desceased. Through the Na'vi avatar, Jake gains the ability to walk again and accidentally falls in love with a Na'vi woman named Neytiri. As Jake and Neytiri continue to grow their love, he is now sucked into saving Pandora before his fellow humans destory it.
