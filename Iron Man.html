Genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist, Tony Stark. He is the son of great inventor Howard Stark and is now CEO of Stark Industries. Soon after his weapons presentation in Iraq, Tony finds himself in attack with results in him recieving a chest full of shrapnel and being kidnapped by terrorists. In order to survive, Tony creates a miniature arc reactor in the cave that he is trapped in. But while he is desperate to escape, Tony creates a suit made entirely of iron and spare parts. From there brings the creation of Iron Man. He uses this suit to escape from the cave and to him eventually leaving Iraq. Once he has been safely returned home, Tony starts getting to work and re-creating and developing the Iron Man suit. From there he continues perfecting it while also running Stark Industries. Eventually he will use this suit to fight of evil and save himslef and his company, but could evil be right under his nose?
