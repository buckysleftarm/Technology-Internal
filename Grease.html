Danny Zuko, leader of Rydell Highs "T-Birds", has fallen in love over summer with Australian Sandy Olsson. As the summer was ending, Sandy and Danny thought they were never going to see each otehr again. However, Sandy and her parents had moved to America and she would attend Rydell High. Sandy finds friends in the schools other group The Pink Ladies. The two lovers find each other again at the pep rally, but Danny seems to have changed from the one she had met at the beach. As time speeds by it seems like one must change for the other. Will Danny drop the greaser image or will sandy drop the good girl image?
