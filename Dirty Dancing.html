Baby Houseman is just one summer away from being at Peace Corps. But she's disappointed when she has too spend her current summer at a sleepy gateway in the Catskills with her parents in tow. However, she turns lucky when she meets the dance instructor, Johnny Castle. Johnny decides to enlist Baby to be his new dance partner. But as they grow closer to each other, they start to fall in love. All goes well except for one thing - Babys' father has forbid her from seeing Johnny. But Baby is determined to help Johnny to perform his biggest dance of the summer and to hopefully grow their romance.
